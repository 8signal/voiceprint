# Voice tics — pending review

Catch file for new voice tics, signature phrases, openers, transitions, coined phrases, banned-AI cousins, and recurring patterns Voiceprint spots in the SME's transcripts but hasn't yet promoted to `identity.md` Section 6.

**The loop (per `rules.md` Rule 11):**

1. Voiceprint reads every uploaded transcript twice. Second pass scans for voice signal.
2. New candidates land here with source pointer and example sentence.
3. When 3+ accumulate, or when a phrase shows up across multiple transcripts (signature signal), Voiceprint surfaces them for SME approval.
4. On approval, promote to `identity.md` Section 6 with a gloss and example. Mark shipped here.

## Categories

- **Openers** — new ways the SME starts thoughts
- **Transitions** — connectives between paragraphs / ideas
- **Coined phrases** — original framings, payoff lines, contrast couplets the SME built themselves
- **Pop culture / metaphor moves** — references and comp moves
- **Spanish / bilingual** — phrases that ship fine in published posts (NSFW phrases stay out)
- **Banned AI cousins** — phrases that *sound* like the SME but aren't, candidates for the banned list
- **Archetype moves** — naming-a-type patterns ("the [X] in their world")
